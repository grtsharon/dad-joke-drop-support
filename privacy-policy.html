Dad Joke Drop Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Dad Joke Drop is a simple entertainment app that works on your device.

Data Collection

Dad Joke Drop does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not use third-party analytics, does not use advertising trackers, and does not send your joke history to a server.

On-Device Data

Dad Joke Drop may store simple app preferences on your device, including whether onboarding has been completed, your selected daily joke notification time, your selected appearance setting, and recent joke history used by the in-app archive.

This information stays on your device and is used only to make the app work.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Dad Joke Drop uses Apple local notifications to remind you when your daily joke is ready. You can turn notifications off at any time in the app settings or in iOS Settings.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change.
